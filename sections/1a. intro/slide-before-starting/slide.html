Before you start!

Only tested for Chrome

A wifi connection is recommended, there are some big images and videos to load

A resolution as close to 1920x1080 as possible gives the sharpest results

Use the arrow keys to switch slides

The "full interactive project" (iframe) slides have arrow marks in the lower right of the page that you can click to move to the next slide (see the example in the corner below)
